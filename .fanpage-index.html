Bio

Blink-182 is an American rock band from Poway, California. They were formed in 1992 by Mark Hoppus and first gained notoriety in the early '90s with their high energy shows. In 2009, after their mainstream breakthrough and eventual hiatus, the band appeared in Germany to announce their reunion. After continued success Tom DeLonge decided to leave the band in 2015 and Matt Skiba was enlisted. Announced on July 25, 2019, "Nine" is their newest album that was released on September 20, 2019.
